Meet Federico

Federico Astiz
Board Of Directors

Federico Astiz was born and raised in Uruguay, South America. He migrated to the US with his family in 1967 and after getting a Bachelor’s degree from the University of Maryland in Geology with a minor in Psychology in 1974, he moved west to Santa Fe, New Mexico.

In 1976, he came in contact with the Pueblo tribes of northern NM, who introduced him to the Native American Church Peyote ceremony thru the American Church of God. He has been a member ever since and has participated in countless peyote ceremonies for almost half a century.

He currently resides in Tucson Arizona with his companion Rainbow Lopez who is a registered member of the Tohono O’Odham tribe and member of the NACSA in Southern Arizona.
